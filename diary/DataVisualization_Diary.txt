1. What is the main story or message your visualizations or article communicate?

   	There are 2 parts of visualizations in the dashboard, Health (AQI and Cancer rate) and Economic (AQI and Income). 
	
	1. Health: Show the relationship between percentages of unhealthy air days and lung cancer incidence rates.
	
	2. Economic: Compare the air quality in different income level counties.

2. Sketch of you dashboard or outline of your article sections
	
   Dashboard 	
	    |--- Tab 1
		      |--- Min Median AQI Slider: User can control the slider to see cancer rate under different AQI.
		      |--- Scatter Plot: Air Quality vs. Cancer.	
		      |--- Histogram: Distribution of Cancer Rates.
 	    |--- Tab 2
		      |--- Income Tier Dropdown: User can select different income tier to see the AQI distribution in different 			                        										 income level.
		      |--- Scatter Plot: Income vs. Air Quality.
              |--- Box Plot: Air Quality Distribution across Income Brackets.
